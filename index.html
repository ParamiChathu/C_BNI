Anamaduwa Land Preparation

We are excited to announce that our 7-acre land in Anamaduwa is now fully prepared for the cultivation of jumbo peanuts and chili peppers. The land, known for its fertile soil and optimal growing conditions, has been carefully prepared to ensure the best possible yield. Our team has worked tirelessly to enhance the soil quality, ensuring it meets the high standards necessary for these crops. As we embark on this new agricultural venture, we are confident that our sustainable farming practices will lead to a successful harvest, contributing to the growth of eco-friendly agriculture in Sri Lanka.
